Mint Check TCG · Mobile app

Privacy Policy

Sweitzer Automations (“we”, “us”) operates the Mint Check TCG application. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the app.

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Overview

Mint Check TCG helps you catalog and evaluate trading cards. Unless you explicitly enable optional features that send data to third-party services, your card photos and notes stay on your device. We do not sell your personal information.

Information the app may access

  • Photos and camera. If you grant permission, the app can capture or import images of cards for your collection. Those images are processed and stored locally unless you use a feature that uploads them (see below).
  • Files and media. You may import images from your device’s photo library or storage where the operating system allows.
  • Optional AI features (“bring your own key”). If you choose to connect an AI provider using your own API key or credentials, requests may be sent directly from your device to that provider according to their terms and privacy policy. We do not receive your API keys when you store them only on your device; traffic goes between your device and the provider you configured.
  • Network. Standard internet access may be used for features you enable (for example, calling an external API you configured, or updating app-related metadata your platform provides). The core cataloging experience can work offline once content is on your device.
  • Contacts. The app does not require access to your contacts. If a future version requested contacts for a specific feature, we would update this policy and the app’s permission prompts before enabling it.

How we use information

We use information only to operate and improve the app experience:

  • To display and organize your collection inside the app.
  • To run optional features you turn on (such as AI-assisted descriptions when you supply your own key).
  • To respond if you contact us for support (see Contact below).

Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal information. Data leaves your device mainly when you enable a feature that talks to an external service—for example, an AI provider with credentials you entered—or when your device vendor or platform handles crash diagnostics or updates according to their settings.

Third-party services you connect (including AI APIs) process data under their own policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any provider you use with Mint Check TCG.

Retention

Collection data you create in the app remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app, unless you use a sync or backup mechanism offered by your platform (for example, device backups). Email messages you send to us are retained only as long as needed to respond and operate our business.

Children

Mint Check TCG is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Security

We design the app so sensitive collection data stays on your device by default. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; use device lock screens and platform backups thoughtfully.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, or to object to certain processing. Because most data stays on your device, you can often delete it directly in the app or by removing the app. To exercise rights relating to information we hold as a business (for example, support emails), contact us below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy where permitted by law.
