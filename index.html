About Sweitzer Automations

We’re a hands-on automation partner focused on control systems that work in the real world—noisy floors, tight windows, and teams who need answers at 2 a.m., not slides on Monday.

Whether you’re modernizing a legacy line or standing up something new, we stay close to operators and maintenance so the solution fits the plant—not just the spec.

We’re anchored in Post Falls, Idaho (ZIP 83854, Kootenai County)—serving manufacturing and industrial teams across North Idaho, the Inland Northwest, and nearby regions, with travel and remote support arranged to match your schedule.

  • Safety & clarity Designs and documentation that respect your safety culture and your next shift.
  • Straight talk Scope and risks spelled out early—fewer surprises when you’re on the clock.
  • Long-term fit Systems your team can run, tune, and extend as production changes.
